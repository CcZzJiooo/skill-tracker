# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project welcomes practical, respectful collaboration.

Examples of good behavior:

- Report problems with reproducible details.
- Disagree with technical reasoning, not personal attacks.
- Respect privacy when sharing logs, screenshots, and telemetry.
- Keep issues focused and actionable.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or insulting comments.
- Publishing another person's private data.
- Posting raw logs that expose secrets or private local paths.
- Repeatedly derailing issues away from the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocking from the repository.
